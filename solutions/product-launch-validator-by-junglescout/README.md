# Product Launch Validator (Uplizd) - Validate product ideas and market viability before investing

## Summary
The Product Launch Validator is an automated AI workflow designed to help product managers and entrepreneurs mitigate risk by analyzing market data, competitor positioning, and demand signals before committing capital to inventory. By leveraging the Composio Toolset to query real-time market insights, the workflow provides a single source of truth for product viability, ensuring that only high-potential concepts move forward in the development pipeline.

## Quick Start
### 1) Import the Flow into Uplizd
1. Click the "Run on Uplizd" badge above to navigate to the solution template.
2. Select "Import" to add the Product Launch Validator workflow to your workspace.
3. Authenticate your required market research and CRM tool connections within the Composio dashboard.
4. Ensure nodes are correctly mapped from Chat Input to the Agent and verify that the output destination is configured.

### 2) Setup the Nodes
- **Chat Input**: Receives the product idea, target category, and initial budget parameters.
- **Agent**: Processes the request, performs deep-web research, and synthesizes market data.
- **Composio Toolset**: Executes API calls to fetch real-time market metrics and competitor data.
- **Chat Output**: Delivers the final validation report, including viability score and strategic recommendations.

### 3) Run the Flow
Open the Playground and test the workflow with these prompts:
- `Analyze the market viability for a new line of eco-friendly yoga mats in the current US market.`
- `Compare the top 3 competitors for 'portable espresso makers' and identify their pricing weaknesses.`
- `Generate a risk assessment report for launching a new kitchen gadget based on current search trends.`

## Configuration
### 1) Language Model (Agent Node)
The agent acts as a strategic product analyst.
- Use a high-reasoning model (e.g., GPT-4o or Claude 3.5 Sonnet) for accurate data synthesis.
- Instruct the agent to prioritize quantitative data over anecdotal evidence.
- Maintain a neutral, analytical tone for all viability reports.

### 2) Composio Toolset Node
- Provide your unique API key to enable secure access to market research integrations.
- Ensure the connection scope includes read-only access to market analytics and search tools.

### 3) Tool Availability
- **Market Search Tools**: For retrieving search volume and trend data.
- **Competitor Intelligence APIs**: For scraping pricing and product feature sets.
- **Data Formatting Tools**: For structuring the final output into readable tables and summaries.
