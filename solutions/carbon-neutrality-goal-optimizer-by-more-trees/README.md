## Quick Start
### 1) Import the Flow into Uplizd
1. Navigate to the Uplizd dashboard and select "Create New Flow."
2. Import the Carbon Neutrality Goal Optimizer template from the marketplace.
3. Authenticate your required environmental data connectors within the Composio configuration.
4. Ensure nodes are correctly mapped from Chat Input to the Agent and final Output.

### 2) Setup the Nodes
- **Chat Input**: Receives user queries regarding emission data or sustainability strategy requests.
- **Agent**: Processes environmental metrics and formulates optimization strategies based on current goals.
- **Composio Toolset**: Executes data retrieval and updates records in your integrated sustainability or ERP platforms.
- **Chat Output**: Delivers clear, actionable insights and progress reports back to the user.

### 3) Run the Flow
Use the Playground to test the workflow with the following prompts:
- `What is our current progress toward our 2025 carbon neutrality goal?`
- `Identify the top three operational areas contributing to our emissions this quarter.`
- `Suggest a strategy to reduce energy consumption in our primary manufacturing facility.`

## Configuration
### 1) Language Model (Agent Node)
The agent acts as a sustainability analyst, interpreting complex environmental data to provide strategic guidance.
- **Recommended instruction pattern:**
    - Act as a sustainability expert with deep knowledge of ESG reporting standards.
    - Prioritize data-driven insights over generic recommendations.
    - Maintain a professional, objective tone suitable for executive reporting.

### 2) Composio Toolset Node
- **API Key**: Ensure your Composio API key is active and authorized for the specific environmental and ERP tools in use.
- **Connection Scope**: Grant read/write access to the relevant data repositories to allow the agent to fetch metrics and log optimization actions.

### 3) Tool Availability
- **Data Retrieval Tools**: Fetching energy usage, waste management, and supply chain emission logs.
- **Analytics Tools**: Processing time-series data to identify trends and anomalies.
- **Reporting Tools**: Formatting insights into structured documents or dashboard updates.
