# Social Proof Journey Mapper (Uplizd) - Automate customer trust tracking and conversion mapping

## Summary
The Social Proof Journey Mapper is an intelligent Uplizd workflow designed to track, analyze, and map how customer interactions with social proof—such as testimonials, reviews, and case studies—influence the buying journey. By automating the correlation between trust-building assets and conversion events, this solution provides marketing and sales teams with a single source of truth regarding which social proof touchpoints drive the highest pipeline velocity and revenue impact.

- **Automated Interaction Mapping**

## Quick Start
### 1) Import the Flow into Uplizd
1. Click the "Run on Uplizd" badge above to open the template in your workspace.
2. Connect your required CRM and marketing analytics accounts via the Composio Toolset.
3. Configure your notification channels (e.g., Slack or Email) to receive journey insights.
4. Ensure nodes are correctly mapped from the Chat Input to the Agent and final Chat Output.

### 2) Setup the Nodes
- **Chat Input**: Receives the prospect identifier or email address to initiate the journey map.
- **Agent**: Processes interaction data and correlates it against conversion milestones.
- **Composio Toolset**: Executes queries to fetch engagement data from your integrated marketing and CRM platforms.
- **Chat Output**: Returns a comprehensive summary of the prospect's social proof journey and recommended next steps.

### 3) Run the Flow
Use the Playground to test the workflow with the following prompts:
- `Map the social proof journey for prospect email: lead@example.com`
- `Which testimonials have the highest impact on conversion for the Enterprise segment?`
- `Identify prospects who have engaged with case studies but haven't booked a demo yet.`

## Configuration
### 1) Language Model (Agent Node)
The agent acts as a marketing analyst, interpreting raw engagement data into actionable insights.
- Instruct the agent to prioritize high-intent interactions over general page views.
- Configure the agent to identify patterns between specific asset types and deal velocity.
- Maintain a professional, data-driven tone for all generated reports and summaries.

### 2) Composio Toolset Node
- Provide your API key to enable secure connections to your CRM (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot) and analytics tools.
- Scope connection permissions to read-only access for analytics data to ensure data hygiene.

### 3) Tool Availability
- **CRM Connector**: Fetch prospect status and deal stages.
- **Analytics Connector**: Retrieve engagement logs and click-through data.
- **Notification Service**: Send alerts to the sales team regarding high-intent engagement.
