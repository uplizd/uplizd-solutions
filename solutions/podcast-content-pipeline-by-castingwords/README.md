## Features
- **Automated Transcription**
  Leverages CastingWords to convert raw audio files into high-accuracy text transcripts for downstream processing.
- **Multi-Format Generation**
  Uses AI agents to synthesize transcripts into blog posts, LinkedIn updates, and newsletter summaries.
- **Composio Toolset Integration**
  Connects the workflow to your content management systems and social media platforms for seamless publishing.
- **Real-Time Content Sync**
  Ensures that once an episode is processed, all derived assets are ready for review or distribution immediately.
- **Customizable Tone Mapping**
  Allows users to define specific brand voices for different output formats, ensuring consistency across all generated content.

## Quick Start
### 1) Import the Flow into Uplizd
1. Click the "Run on Uplizd" badge above to open the solution template.
2. Select your preferred workspace to import the workflow.
3. Authenticate your CastingWords and social media/CMS accounts via the Composio Toolset.
4. Ensure nodes are correctly mapped to your specific API credentials and output folders.

### 2) Setup the Nodes
*   **Chat Input**: Receives the audio file link or episode metadata.
*   **Agent**: Processes the transcript and executes the content generation logic.
*   **Composio Toolset**: Connects to external platforms to fetch transcripts and push generated content.
*   **Chat Output**: Delivers the final drafted content assets for your review.

### 3) Run the Flow
Access the Playground in your Uplizd dashboard and try these prompts:
* `Generate a LinkedIn post and a 500-word blog summary for the latest episode URL provided.`
* `Create a newsletter draft highlighting the three most important points from the transcript.`
* `Extract the top 5 quotes from the podcast and format them for Twitter/X.`

## Configuration
### 1) Language Model (Agent Node)
The agent acts as your content strategist and copywriter.
*   **Recommended instruction pattern:**
    *   "Act as a professional content marketer specializing in repurposing audio content."
    *   "Maintain a professional yet engaging tone consistent with our brand guidelines."
    *   "Prioritize clarity and SEO-friendly headings in all generated blog content."

### 2) Composio Toolset Node
*   **API Key:** Ensure your CastingWords and CMS API keys are stored securely in the Composio dashboard.
*   **Connection Scope:** Grant read access for audio retrieval and write access for your blog/social platforms.

### 3) Tool Availability
*   **CastingWords API**: For high-quality audio transcription.
*   **CMS Connectors**: For automated blog post creation (e.g., WordPress, Webflow).
*   **Social Media APIs**: For automated snippet distribution.
