## Features
- **Relationship Strength Scoring**
    - Automatically calculates interaction frequency and recency to rank the quality of professional connections.
- **Warm Path Identification**
    - Uses AI to traverse your network and suggest the most effective "bridge" contact for reaching a target prospect.
- **CRM Integration**
    - Syncs relationship insights directly back to your CRM via the Composio Toolset for unified account views.
- **Real-time Network Analysis**
    - Processes updates to your professional network in real-time to ensure introduction paths are always current.
- **Automated Outreach Context**
    - Generates personalized introduction templates that highlight the specific connection point between the parties.

## Quick Start
### 1) Import the Flow into Uplizd
1. Navigate to the Uplizd marketplace and locate the Network Relationship Mapper.
2. Click "Import" to add the workflow to your workspace.
3. Connect your CRM and Affinity accounts within the integration settings.
4. Ensure nodes are correctly mapped to your specific CRM field names and API credentials.

### 2) Setup the Nodes
- **Chat Input**: Receives the target account or prospect name from the user.
- **Agent**: Analyzes the request and queries the network data for connection paths.
- **Composio Toolset**: Executes API calls to fetch relationship strength and contact metadata.
- **Chat Output**: Displays the recommended introduction path and suggested outreach message.

### 3) Run the Flow
Use the Playground to test your network mapping:
- `Find the best person at Acme Corp to introduce me to the VP of Engineering.`
- `Who in our network has the strongest relationship with the CEO of Global Tech?`
- `Generate a warm introduction email for a prospect identified in our current network.`

## Configuration
### 1) Language Model (Agent Node)
The Agent acts as the analytical engine for your professional network.
- **Role**: Act as a Sales Intelligence Analyst.
- **Instruction Pattern**:
    - Prioritize connections with high interaction frequency and recent communication.
    - Maintain strict privacy by only suggesting paths authorized by your organization's sharing settings.
    - Provide concise, actionable summaries of why a specific contact is the best bridge.

### 2) Composio Toolset Node
- **API Key**: Ensure your Affinity and CRM API keys are active in the Composio dashboard.
- **Connection Scope**: Grant read-only access to contact lists and interaction history to ensure data security.

### 3) Tool Availability
- **Network Query**: Fetches connection strength and interaction metadata.
- **CRM Lookup**: Retrieves account-level details and stakeholder roles.
- **Message Generator**: Drafts personalized outreach content based on the identified path.
