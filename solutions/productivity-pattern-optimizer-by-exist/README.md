## Quick Start
### 1) Import the Flow into Uplizd
1. Navigate to the Uplizd marketplace and locate the Productivity Pattern Optimizer.
2. Click "Import" to add the workflow template to your workspace.
3. Connect your required data sources (e.g., Calendar, Task Manager) via the Composio Toolset.
4. Ensure nodes are correctly mapped and all API credentials are authenticated in the configuration panel.

### 2) Setup the Nodes
*   **Chat Input**: Receives your natural language queries regarding your productivity data.
*   **Agent**: Processes activity logs and applies optimization logic to identify patterns.
*   **Composio Toolset**: Executes data retrieval and task updates across your connected productivity apps.
*   **Chat Output**: Delivers clear, summarized insights and actionable recommendations to your interface.

### 3) Run the Flow
Use the Playground to test your workflow with these example prompts:
* `Analyze my last week of work and identify the top three tasks that caused the most context switching.`
* `Based on my historical completion rates, suggest a more efficient schedule for my upcoming project tasks.`
* `Which recurring meetings are currently consuming the most time without delivering clear project value?`

## Configuration
### 1) Language Model (Agent Node)
The Agent acts as your personal productivity analyst, synthesizing logs into strategy.
*   Maintain a neutral, analytical tone when presenting performance data.
*   Prioritize actionable insights over raw data dumps.
*   Always link recommendations back to specific historical patterns found in the input data.

### 2) Composio Toolset Node
*   **API Key**: Ensure your Composio API key is active with read/write permissions for your productivity suite.
*   **Connection Scope**: Grant access to your primary calendar and task management platforms to ensure comprehensive data coverage.

### 3) Tool Availability
*   **Calendar API**: For tracking meeting duration and frequency.
*   **Task Manager API**: For monitoring task creation, status, and completion timestamps.
*   **Communication Log API**: For analyzing time spent in messaging platforms.
